{{__ "Collaborative Learning Through Assessment (CLASS)"}}

{{__ "Traditionally, students only solve problems. In the CLASS system, students learn so much more by engaging with more stages of an assignment."}}


{{__ "Here's the process you'll follow. Everything shows as anonymous"}}:

  • {{__ "Each student creates a problem according to the instructions"}}

  • {{__ "The instructor optionally edits the problem to ensure quality"}}

  • {{__ "Another student solves the problem"}}

  • {{__ "Two students grade the solution, including the problem creator"}}

  • {{__ "If the graders disagree, another student resolves the grade"}}

  • {{__ "Optionally, the problem solver can dispute the grade"}}

  • {{__ "The instructor resolves any disputes"}}

  • {{__ "Students can see everything their peers have done anonymously"}}
